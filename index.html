Visa & Residence Permit Guide

Short practical steps for international students coming to Turkey.

  1. Apply for a student visa at the nearest Turkish consulate. Prepare acceptance letter, passport, financial proof, and health insurance.
  2. On arrival, register your address and complete the residence permit application via e-ikamet (e-government) within the first 30 days.
  3. Keep copies of all documents and attend the International Office for support.

Downloadable checklist: Passport copy, University acceptance letter, Passport photos, Proof of accommodation, Health insurance, Financial proof.

  • Consider proximity to campus and transport
